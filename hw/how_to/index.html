How to Recycle in simple steps!

Welcome to the green tutorial for how to recycle properly.

Today you will learn how to help the earth in simple steps.
Click on the image to begin

  • First decide if the item you want to recycle can be reused for something else. For example you may want to reuse a glass bottle or a bag if not countinue onto the next step.

  • See if your item can be recycled based on where you live. This step is critical because recycling rules vary greatly depending on where you live. For example Miami uses a single stream recyling which makes the next step critical. If you are unsure use your computer or smart phone to check online.

  • Clean the item you want to recyle. In areas with single stream recyling such as Miami if you try and recyle a plastic soda bottle without rinsing it out first it will not be recyled.

  • Place your item in the recyling bin!

  • Feel good about helping out the earth and the people who live in it! Countinue to follow the recyling steps and encourage others to become concious of their recycling habiats so they too can help the earth.
